Help Center

Find answers to frequently asked questions

How do I get started?

Create a free account by clicking "Register" on the homepage. Verify your email, deposit funds, and start playing Chess Duels for real money!

How do I deposit money?

Go to your Dashboard and click "Deposit". We accept multiple payment methods including cryptocurrency and credit cards. Minimum deposit is $5.

How fast are withdrawals?

Crypto withdrawals are processed within 24 hours. Credit card withdrawals may take 3-5 business days depending on your bank.

What is the minimum withdrawal amount?

The minimum withdrawal amount is $10 for cryptocurrency and $20 for credit cards.

How does matchmaking work?

Our system pairs you with opponents of similar skill levels and bet amounts. All matches are 1v1 against real players to ensure fair gameplay.

Is my personal information safe?

Yes! We use military-grade encryption (SSL/TLS) to protect all user data. Your information is stored securely on enterprise cloud infrastructure.

What happens if I lose connection during a game?

If you disconnect during a match, you have 60 seconds to reconnect. After that, the game may be forfeited depending on the situation.

Can I play for free?

Yes! We offer practice modes where you can play without risking real money to improve your skills before competing for cash.

What games are available?

Currently, Chess Duels is live and fully operational. More skill-based games including Card Battles and Strategy Games are coming soon!

How do I report a problem?

Contact our support team through the "Contact Us" page or email us directly. We respond to all inquiries within 24 hours.
