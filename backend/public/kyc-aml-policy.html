KYC & AML Policy

Know Your Customer & Anti-Money Laundering Compliance

⚠️ Important: Chess Duelz is committed to preventing money laundering, terrorist financing, and other illicit activities. We comply with applicable KYC (Know Your Customer) and AML (Anti-Money Laundering) regulations.

1. Policy Overview

This KYC & AML Policy outlines our commitment to preventing financial crimes and ensuring compliance with applicable laws and regulations. By using Chess Duelz, you agree to cooperate with our verification procedures.

Our Commitment

  • Verify the identity of all users
  • Monitor and report suspicious activities
  • Comply with applicable financial regulations
  • Maintain secure records of user information
  • Prevent use of our platform for illegal purposes

2. Know Your Customer (KYC)

Identity Verification Requirements

All users must complete identity verification before they can:

  • Withdraw funds from their account
  • Make deposits exceeding $500 in a single transaction
  • Accumulate total deposits exceeding $1,000
  • Participate in high-stakes games (entry fee > $100)

Required Documentation

To verify your identity, you must provide:

  • Government-Issued ID: Valid passport, driver's license, or national ID card
  • Proof of Address: Utility bill, bank statement, or government correspondence (issued within last 3 months)
  • Selfie Verification: Photo of yourself holding your ID document

Verification Process

  1. Submit required documents through your account dashboard
  2. Our compliance team reviews your submission (typically within 24-48 hours)
  3. You'll receive email notification of approval or request for additional information
  4. Once approved, your account will have full access to all features

Document Standards

  • Documents must be clear, legible, and in color
  • All four corners of documents must be visible
  • Information must not be obscured or edited
  • Documents must be current and not expired
  • Accepted file formats: JPG, PNG, PDF
  • Maximum file size: 10MB per document

3. Anti-Money Laundering (AML)

Prohibited Activities

The following activities are strictly prohibited on Chess Duelz:

  • Using the platform to launder money or proceeds of crime
  • Financing terrorism or other illegal activities
  • Structuring deposits to avoid reporting thresholds
  • Using accounts on behalf of third parties without disclosure
  • Providing false or misleading information
  • Using VPNs or proxies to hide true location

Transaction Monitoring

We actively monitor all transactions for suspicious activity, including:

  • Unusually large or frequent deposits/withdrawals
  • Patterns inconsistent with normal gaming behavior
  • Rapid deposit-and-withdrawal cycles
  • Multiple accounts linked to the same person or device
  • Transactions involving high-risk jurisdictions

Enhanced Due Diligence

In certain cases, we may require additional verification:

  • Total account activity exceeding $10,000
  • Large withdrawals (over $5,000)
  • Unusual transaction patterns
  • Users from high-risk jurisdictions
  • Politically Exposed Persons (PEPs)

4. Sanctions Screening

We screen all users against international sanctions lists, including:

  • OFAC (Office of Foreign Assets Control) SDN List
  • UN Security Council Sanctions List
  • EU Consolidated Financial Sanctions List
  • Interpol and national watchlists

Users found on sanctions lists will be immediately banned and funds frozen pending legal review.

5. Source of Funds

For large deposits or withdrawals, we may request documentation proving the legitimate source of funds, such as:

  • Employment income (payslips, employment contract)
  • Business income (tax returns, bank statements)
  • Investment income (brokerage statements)
  • Inheritance or gift documentation
  • Sale of property or assets

6. Suspicious Activity Reporting

Internal Review

Our compliance team reviews flagged transactions and may:

  • Request additional documentation
  • Temporarily freeze account pending investigation
  • Limit transaction amounts
  • Close account and return funds if suspicious activity is confirmed

Reporting to Authorities

We are legally required to report suspicious activities to relevant authorities. We will not notify you if a report has been filed, as doing so may compromise investigations.

7. Data Protection & Privacy

All personal information collected for KYC/AML purposes is:

  • Stored securely using industry-standard encryption
  • Accessed only by authorized compliance personnel
  • Retained for the legally required period (typically 5 years after account closure)
  • Protected under our Privacy Policy
  • Never sold or shared with third parties except as required by law

8. Restricted Jurisdictions

Due to regulatory requirements, Chess Duelz is not available to residents of certain jurisdictions. Users from the following locations are prohibited:

  • Countries under international sanctions (e.g., North Korea, Iran)
  • Jurisdictions where skill-based gaming for money is illegal
  • US states with specific prohibitions (check state laws)
  • Any jurisdiction where our services would violate local laws

It is your responsibility to ensure that your use of Chess Duelz complies with your local laws.

9. Account Freezing & Termination

Grounds for Action

We reserve the right to freeze or terminate your account if:

  • You fail to complete KYC verification within 30 days of request
  • You provide false or fraudulent information
  • We detect suspicious activity or money laundering
  • You are found on sanctions lists
  • Legal authorities request action
  • You violate our Terms of Service

Frozen Funds

If your account is frozen:

  • You will not be able to deposit, play, or withdraw
  • Funds may be held pending investigation
  • Legitimate funds will be returned after verification (minus any fees or penalties)
  • Proceeds of illegal activity will not be returned and may be seized by authorities

10. Compliance Updates

We may update this KYC & AML Policy to reflect:

  • Changes in applicable laws and regulations
  • New compliance requirements
  • Enhanced security measures
  • Industry best practices

Significant changes will be communicated via email. Continued use of the platform after changes constitutes acceptance.

Need Help with Verification?

If you have questions about our KYC/AML procedures or need assistance with verification:

  • Email: compliance@treasurehunt.com
  • Support: Contact our support team
  • Response Time: Typically 24-48 hours for verification reviews

Important: Never share your account credentials or verification documents with anyone claiming to be from Chess Duelz via unofficial channels. We will only request documents through your account dashboard or official email.
