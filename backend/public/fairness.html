Fairness & Fair Play

Our commitment to transparent and fair competitive gaming

100% Skill-Based Gaming

Chess Duelz is committed to providing a completely skill-based gaming platform. Unlike games of chance, our games are determined entirely by player skill, strategy, and decision-making.

Core Principle: Every game outcome is determined by player skill, not random chance or house advantage.

What Makes Us Fair?

  • No House Edge: We don't benefit from player losses. We earn through small platform fees only.
  • Equal Starting Conditions: All players start each match with identical conditions and opportunities.
  • Transparent Rules: Game rules are clearly stated and apply equally to all players.
  • Real Opponents: You always play against real human players, never bots or AI.

Fair Matchmaking

Our matchmaking system is designed to pair players of similar skill levels to ensure competitive and enjoyable matches.

How Matchmaking Works:

  • Skill-Based Pairing: Players are matched based on their skill rating and game history.
  • Bet Amount Matching: You're paired with players wagering similar amounts.
  • Real-Time Queue: Live matching system connects you with available players instantly.
  • No Manipulation: The system is automated and cannot be influenced by staff or other players.

Game Integrity

We maintain the highest standards of game integrity through multiple safeguards:

Anti-Cheating Measures:

  • Advanced monitoring systems detect suspicious play patterns
  • Move timing analysis identifies potential computer assistance
  • Account verification prevents multi-accounting abuse
  • Automated and manual review of flagged matches

Security & Fair Play:

  • All game moves are recorded and can be reviewed
  • Encrypted connections prevent tampering during gameplay
  • Independent audits verify system fairness
  • Clear consequences for policy violations

Transparent Operations

We believe in complete transparency about how our platform operates:

  • Platform Fees: We charge a small service fee (typically 5-10%) on game entries, clearly disclosed before play.
  • Payout Rates: 90-95% of all entry fees are returned to winners as prizes.
  • Withdrawal Times: Clearly stated processing times for all withdrawal methods.
  • Game Statistics: Players can view complete match history and statistics.
Our Promise: We never hide fees, manipulate outcomes, or use unfair practices. Your success depends entirely on your skill.

Reporting Concerns

If you ever suspect unfair play or have concerns about fairness, we want to hear from you:

  • Report suspicious behavior through the in-game report system
  • Contact support with detailed information about your concern
  • All reports are investigated thoroughly and confidentially
  • We take action against confirmed violations
