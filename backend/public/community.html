Community Guidelines

To maintain a positive and welcoming environment, all community members must:

  • Be respectful and courteous to all members
  • Avoid spam, advertising, or self-promotion
  • No harassment, hate speech, or discriminatory language
  • Keep discussions relevant to Chess Duelz and competitive chess
  • Respect privacy - no sharing of personal information
  • Follow moderator instructions
  • Report violations to moderators

Violations of these guidelines may result in warnings, temporary suspension, or permanent ban from our community channels.
