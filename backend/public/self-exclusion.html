Self-Exclusion

Take control of your gaming by temporarily or permanently excluding yourself from Chess Duelz

⚠️ Important: Self-exclusion is a serious step. Once activated, you will not be able to log in or play during the exclusion period. Please consider this carefully.

What is Self-Exclusion?

Self-exclusion allows you to block yourself from accessing Chess Duelz for a specified period. This tool is designed to help players who feel they need a break from competitive chess.

During self-exclusion:

  • You will not be able to log into your account
  • You cannot deposit funds or play games
  • Marketing communications will be stopped
  • You can withdraw any existing balance
  • The exclusion cannot be reversed once activated
