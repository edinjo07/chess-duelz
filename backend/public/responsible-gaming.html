Responsible Gaming

Play smart, play safe, play responsibly

⚠️ Important: You must be 18 years or older to play on Chess Duelz. Competitive chess should be entertainment, not a way to make money or solve financial problems.

Our Commitment

Chess Duelz is committed to promoting responsible gaming practices. While our platform offers skill-based competitive chess for real money, we recognize that some players may develop unhealthy gaming habits.

We provide tools and resources to help you stay in control of your gaming experience and ensure it remains fun and entertaining.

Signs of Problem Gaming

Gaming becomes a problem when it interferes with your daily life. Be aware of these warning signs:

  • Spending more money than you can afford to lose
  • Chasing losses by playing more to win back money
  • Neglecting work, school, or family responsibilities
  • Borrowing money to fund your gaming
  • Feeling anxious, irritable, or depressed about gaming
  • Lying to others about your gaming activities
  • Gaming as an escape from personal problems
  • Unable to stop or reduce gaming despite wanting to

If you recognize any of these signs in yourself or someone you know, please seek help.

Responsible Gaming Tips

Set Limits

  • Budget: Only play with money you can afford to lose
  • Time: Set time limits for your gaming sessions
  • Losses: Decide in advance how much you're willing to lose
  • Wins: Set a target and stop when you reach it

Smart Gaming Habits

  • Never chase losses by increasing bets
  • Take regular breaks during gaming sessions
  • Don't play when emotional, tired, or under the influence
  • Keep gaming as entertainment, not a source of income
  • Balance gaming with other activities and responsibilities

Financial Safety

  • Never borrow money to fund gaming
  • Only deposit amounts you can afford to lose
  • Keep track of your deposits and withdrawals
  • Never gamble with money needed for essential expenses

Account Controls

We provide several tools to help you manage your gaming:

Available Controls:

  • Deposit Limits: Set daily, weekly, or monthly deposit limits
  • Loss Limits: Set maximum loss amounts for specific time periods
  • Session Time Limits: Set reminders or automatic logouts
  • Self-Exclusion: Temporarily or permanently close your account
  • Reality Check: Get periodic notifications about time spent playing

Manage Your Limits

Self-Exclusion

If you feel your gaming is becoming problematic, you can self-exclude from Chess Duelz:

  • Temporary Exclusion: Block access for 24 hours, 7 days, 30 days, or 90 days
  • Permanent Exclusion: Close your account permanently

During self-exclusion, you will not be able to log in, deposit, or play. Any existing funds can be withdrawn.
